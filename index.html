Mohammad is currently a Ph.D. Candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at McGill University working with Professor Aditya Mahajan. He received his B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from the Isfahan University of Technology in 2010 and 2012. His principal research interests include decentralized stochastic control, team theory, Robotics and reinforcement learning. He is interested in both theory and applications of Reinforcement learning. Since 2010, he is voluntarily active in the IEEE.
